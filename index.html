About

If life were a multiverse, I'd be the version of me that's always cooking, coding, or solving for x. Welcome to my little corner of the cosmos!

With 20 years of experience in software development and over a decade as a CTO, I've spent my career building and scaling startups, often across geographically distributed setups. I've learned to thrive in chaos. Whether it's scaling a startup across galaxies or debugging code that feels like it came from a parallel universe, I love turning ambitious ideas into real, impactful solutions. I love challenges unique to startups and love leading teams to deliver impactful solutions, transforming chaos into code and ideas into products.

Math isn't just numbers—it's the cheat code to understanding the universe. I've been obsessed with its overlap with computer science since I was a wee Morty. Whether teaching others how math solves seemingly unsolvable problems or geeking out over elegant proofs, I see math as the genius behind every great solution.

Food is my passport to the world. Whether sampling street food in Bangkok or enjoying a taco truck in Austin, I travel far and wide to explore cuisines that help me connect with cultures. It's also how I met and bonded with my wife, proving that good food really does bring people together. When I'm not eating, I'm in the kitchen experimenting with fermentation, pickling, or deep-frying something delicious.
